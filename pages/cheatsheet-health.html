💡 Bag snatching: The most common theft in Saigon is motorbike bag snatching. Carry bags away from the road side and keep your phone in your pocket while walking. If robbed, go directly to the nearest police station — you'll need a report for insurance.

💡 International hospitals in Vietnam: FV Hospital and Vinmec in Saigon · Vinmec and French Hospital in Hanoi. These have English-speaking staff and Western-standard care. Save the address before you travel.
The pattern for all health complaints: Tôi bị... (toy bee) = "I have / I am suffering from..."
Learn this structure and you can describe almost any symptom just by adding the condition.

💡 Traveler's diarrhea is the most common health issue in Vietnam. Stay hydrated, carry oral rehydration salts, and know where the nearest nhà thuốc (pharmacy) is. Most remedies are available over the counter.

💡 Vietnamese pharmacies (nhà thuốc) are everywhere — look for the green cross sign. Many medicines available by prescription in Western countries can be bought over the counter. Bring your medication name written in English; staff will usually recognize brand names.
