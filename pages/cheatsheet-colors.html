📐 How Adjectives Work in Vietnamese
Key rule: In Vietnamese, adjectives come AFTER the noun — opposite of English.

🇺🇸 English: red shirt (adjective first)
🇻🇳 Vietnamese: áo đỏ (shirt red) — noun first

More examples:
"big house" = nhà to (house big)
"beautiful woman" = người đẹp (person beautiful)
"new phone" = điện thoại mới (phone new)
💡 Adding "very" and "too":
lắm (lahm) = very → "đẹp lắm" = very beautiful
quá (kwah) = too/so → "đắt quá" = too expensive / "đẹp quá" = so beautiful
Both go AFTER the adjective.
