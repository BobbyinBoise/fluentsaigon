💡 Navigation tip: Vietnamese people navigate by landmarks, not street numbers. "Turn left at the big tree" is a perfectly normal direction. When lost, show your phone with Google Maps open — locals are happy to help point the way.

💡 Grab is king. Always use Grab or another app in Vietnam — prices are fixed, routes are tracked, and you can show the driver the destination on your phone. Avoid unlicensed taxi touts at airports.

🚨 Emergency Numbers — Save These Now
Police: 113  ·  Fire: 114  ·  Ambulance: 115
These numbers work anywhere in Vietnam. Save them before you travel.
