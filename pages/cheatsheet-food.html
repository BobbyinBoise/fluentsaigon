💡 Ordering pho: Specify the protein — phở bò (beef) or phở gà (chicken). In Saigon, phở comes with a plate of fresh herbs, bean sprouts, and lime on the side.

💡 Nước mắm is everything. Vietnamese food relies on fish sauce the way Italian food relies on olive oil. The best comes from Phú Quốc island — look for it in any decent restaurant.
